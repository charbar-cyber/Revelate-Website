About Revelate

The story behind an AI faith companion built to help people draw closer to God in their everyday life.

Our Story

Born from a belief that AI can help people draw closer to God

Revelate started with a simple conviction: AI could be one of the most powerful tools the church has seen for spiritual growth—if it's built carefully, grounded in Scripture, and shaped around the actual life of the user.

Most faith apps treat people the same. Revelate is the opposite. From the first sign-up, you tell Revi how you want to be guided—Mentor, Friend, Scholar, or Prophet—and the AI shapes its tone, depth, and challenge accordingly.

What you get is a daily rhythm of Scripture and prayer, a companion you can talk to about anything, and on-demand sermons and podcasts on any topic that's on your heart. All of it personal. All of it scripture-grounded. All of it built to walk with you.
