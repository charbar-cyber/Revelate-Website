About Revelate

The story behind faith-based journaling for clarity, peace, and intentional living.

Our Story

Born from a personal need for clarity

Revelate began as a personal tool. Charlie was frustrated by how fragmented reflection, faith, goal-setting, and decision-making had become—spread across notebooks, apps, and mental clutter.

He wanted a single, intentional space to think clearly, journal honestly, and receive guidance grounded in truth rather than noise.

What started as a private system evolved into Revelate: a guided journaling and reflection platform designed to help people slow down, think clearly, and align their actions with their values—using modern technology without losing depth or meaning.

CB

Charlie Barmore

Founder & Creator

A CPA and product builder who has spent the last decade working at the intersection of business, technology, and personal development.

Revelate is currently founder-led, with development support from a small group of independent designers and engineers. The product is intentionally built lean, focused, and user-first.

Find clarity through faith-based journaling, reflection, and Scripture-grounded guidance. Your journey toward deeper faith starts here.
