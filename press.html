About Revelate

An AI faith companion for everyday spiritual growth

Revelate is an AI faith companion designed to help users draw closer to God in their everyday life. The app combines a chat-based AI mentor (Revi) with on-demand sermon and podcast generation, a daily Scripture rhythm, a Bible reader, and guided study plans—all personalized to the user's chosen guidance style and growth areas.

Built by Ledger Labs LLC, Revelate combines modern AI with timeless biblical truth—using thoughtful design to support spiritual growth without replacing Scripture, prayer, or church community.

Your daily AI faith companion. Chat with Revi, generate sermons and podcasts, and build a Scripture-grounded rhythm of growth.
