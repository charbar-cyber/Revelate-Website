Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Revelate ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information.

1 Information We Collect

We may collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, account details.
  • Usage Data: Device type, app interactions, IP address.
  • Content Data: Journal entries, mood selections, scripture preferences.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize the Service.
  • Send push notifications or SMS messages (via Twilio).
  • Improve the app's features and functionality.
  • Respond to user inquiries and support requests.

3 How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it only with:

  • Service providers (e.g., Twilio for messaging, hosting providers).
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4 Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using encryption and industry-standard protections. While we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information, no system is 100% secure.

5 Your Rights

You may:

  • Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Ask us to delete your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of notifications.

6 Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7 Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app with the updated effective date.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, contact info@revelate.co
